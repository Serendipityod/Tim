Love and Warmth


Even though Tim Burton's works are never short of all kinds of weirdo. But at the same time, there are also many warmth and light in his movies, just like Edward is only silently in love with that girl, while the strange dog Mars saved the little boy with his life, Alice saved the wonderland from red queen. The weirder Tim Burton's works are ,the warmer they could be.


Perhaps the most valuable thing about Tim Burton is that there are always hope, love and warmth hidden in his scary appearance. While, whether it's in movies, or the journey of life, this is probably one of the things that really sustains us and keep us going.
